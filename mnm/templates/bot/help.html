Using the stats bot

The stats bot offer a complementary way to interact with the MNM project directly from your Mastodon account:

  • To quickly get and share some data about users, trending hashtags or instances...
  • To subscribe to daily updates about important metrics of the network

In order to use it, you simply need to mention the MNM bot from you mastodon account, like so: "@{{ bot_name }} /command". Of course, you will have to replace "/command" with an actual command. Available commands are listed below.

This bot is stil in early development and more feature will be added soon!
